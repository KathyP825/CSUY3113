-   get the top left and bottom right corners

-   need to watch out for:
    -   need to take the position you are in
    -   top left corner is 1/2 the width & 1/2 the height 
    -   bottom right corner is 1/2 the width & 1/2 the height 
        -   add width, then subtract height
    -   need to take your position and adjust for that
